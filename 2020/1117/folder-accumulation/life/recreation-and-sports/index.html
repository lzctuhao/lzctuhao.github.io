话题下的句型使用

  1. 很显然，音乐是快乐和灵感的源泉，是我们生活中不可或缺的一部分。（主语从句、同位语）
    It is self-evident that music, the source of joy and inspiration, plays an essential role in our life.

  2. 学校设有两个操场和一个华丽的体育场，使我们可以方便地参加各种体育活动。（非谓语动词）
    Equipped with two playgrounds as well as a splendid stadium, our school offers us easy access to a wide range of sports.

  3. 我们不仅参加篮球、足球和排球课程，学校还举办各种体育比赛，其中篮球和足球比赛非常受欢迎。（Not only … but also … ，定语从句)
    Not only do we take basketball, football and volleyball courses, but our school also holds all kinds of sports competitions, of which basketball and football matches enjoy tremendous popularity.

  4. 就我而言，打排球是最好的，因为它能增强我的团队精神，给我力量去面对生活中的挑战。（否定比较级表示最高级）
    As far as I’m concerned, there is nothing better than playing volleyball since it strengthens my teamwork spirit and gives me strength to confront the challenges in my life.

  5. 汤姆虽然受了伤，但他坚持完成了比赛，赢得了我们的尊重和掌声。(倒装结构、定语从句)
    Injured as he was, Tom persevered/persisted in finishing the race, which won our respect as well as applause.
