A. 常用谚语（在议论文中）

  1. As a popular saying goes, ‘Every coin has two sides’.
  2. As a proverb says, ‘Everything has two side’. On the one hand, physical exercise is good for your health. On the other hand, if you don’t deal with it properly, it will do harm to your health. So we should look at the matter from two sides.
  3. As a proverb says, "Where there is a will there is a way."（有志者事竟成）
  4. As a popular saying goes, "A man is known by the company he keeps."（观其交友，知其为人）
  5. As is known to all, "No pains, no gains". （没有苦，就没有甜）

B. 用于主题、结论句

  1. (It is) needless to say (that) + 子句 = It is obvious/clear that + 子句 = Obviously, S. + V. + 子句 = There is no doubt that ...

  2. There is no= There is no way of = There is no possibility of = lt is impossible to ... = lt is out of the question to ... = No one can ... = We cannot ... 是不可能的；无法

    There is not denying that successful business lies in a healthy body and mind.

  3. Among various kinds of …, …= Of all the … 在各种……之中

    Among various kinds of sports, I like jogging in particular.

  4. It can be easily proved (that) + 子句 ...是很容易证明的

  5. …cannot be overemphasized 无论如何强调都不为过

    The importance of traffic safety cannot be overemphasized.

  6. Everyone knows (that) 子句 每个人都知道...

  7. There is no doubt (that) + 子句 毫无疑问地...

    There is no doubt that near-sightedness is a serious problem among the youth of our country.

  8. According to my personal experience, … = Based on my personal experience, … 根据我个人经验

    According to my personal experience, smile has done me a lot of good.

  9. Of all the people I know, perhaps non deserves my respect more than… 在我认识的人当中也许没有一个人比...更值得我尊敬 。

    Of all the people I know, perhaps non deserves my respect more than Miss Chang, my English teacher.

  10. In the course of my schooling, I will never forget… 在我的求学过程中我忘不了

    In the course of my schooling. I will never forget the great difficulty I encountered in learning English.

    1. With the increase/growth of the population,… 随着人口的增加

    2. With the advance of science and technology,… 随着科技的进步

  12. Recently, the phenomenon that …… has aroused wide public concern. 最近，XXXXX 现象引起了人们的广泛关注

    Recently, the phenomenon that CPI keeps increasing has aroused wide public concern.

    1. In the age of information and communication, … plays an important role. 在这信息的年代扮演重要的角色 。

    2. In today's industrial society, … is indispensable to life. 在今日工业社会中，...是生命不可或缺的 。

    In this age of information and communication, the computer plays an extremely important role.

  14. In dealing with , one cannot but admit (that) + 子句 在讨论...时，不得不承认....

    In dealing with one's future career, one cannot but admit that it is very important to decide one's future career as early as possible.

  15. Nothing in the world can delight me so much as … 世上没有什么比...更令我高兴 。

  16. It is necessary that S (should) V … It is important/essential that S (should) V … It is proper that S (should) V …
    It is urgent that S (should) V …

    It is proper that we (should) keep the public places clean.

  17. Whenever I hear / do / think of / meet with / see… , I cannot but feel excited/ sad/ nervous/ frightened/ surprised.

    Whenever I think of the clean brook near my home, I cannot but feel sad. = Every time I think of the clean brook near my home, I cannot help feeling sad.

  18. It is said / thought / known / reported / expected / estimated / believed (that) 子句

    It is believed (that) reading increases our knowledge and broadens our mind.

  19. Well goes an old saying, ‘…’ = As an old saying goes (runs, says), ‘…’ = An old saying goes, ‘…’ = It's an old saying (that) 俗话说得好...

    1. …in the following ways. ...方法

    2. …for three major reasons. ...有三个主要理由

    I increase my confidence in the following ways. 我用下列方法增加信心 。

    People learn a foreign language for three major reasons. 人们学外语有三个理由 。

  21. If one can really put the three points into action (practice), … 如果能实践这三点

    To…, there are at least three things we can do. 要...至少我们可做三件事 。

    By putting them (the above) into practice, ….
    实践这些……

    If one can really put the three points into action(practice), he will surely be able to live a healthy and happy life.

  22. By doing these simple things, we surely can … 做这些简单之事 ， 我们一定可以

    By doing these simple things, we surely can go out of the door happily and come back home safe every day. 做这些简单之事 ， 我们一定可以快乐出门平安回家 。

    1. Only by living up to the three requirements, can we …. 唯有符合此三项要求 ， 我们才能...

    2. Only with combined efforts, can we …. 唯有通力合作 ， 我们才能...

    Only with combined efforts, can we expect Taiwan to take a new face in due course.

  24. This evidence shows that the importance of … cannot be overemphasized. 这证据显示......的重要性在怎么强调都不为过 。

    Along with the advance of the society (science and technology), people are 随着社会 （ 科技 ） 的发展 ， 人们开始注意到...的重要性

    This evidence shows that the importance Of traffic safety cannot be overemphasized. 这证据显示交通安全的重要性在怎么强调都不为过 。

  25. Currently, there is an inevitable and undeniable fact that …… At present, it is inevitable and undeniable that…… 现在 （ 目前 ), XXXX 见象是不可避免且难以否认的

    Currently, there is an inevitable and undeniable fact that computer is playing a significant role in our daily lives.

    At present, it is inevitable and undeniable that advertisement has been flooding into our daily lives.

  26. If we can do as mentioned above, there can be no doubt (that)

  27. Thus, this is the reason why ….

  28. Therefore, we should realize (that)

    1. From the point of view, ….

  30. According to point of view, ….

  31. An advantage of … is that + …… ……

  32. So +

be +

that +

  1. Adj. + as + Subject + be + V…

  3. By +V-ing …can… …… …… ……

  4. …enable + Object + to + V …… …… …… On no account can we + V …

  5. It is time + sb. +
    =It is high time that we put considerable/great/special emphasis on ...

  6. Spare no effort to + V

  7. bring home to +

  8. be closely related to ~~

  9. Get into the habit of + doing = make it a rule to +v.

  10. Due to/Owing to/Thanks to + N/doing, …

  11. do good to …… do harm to

  12. Pose a great threat to…

  13. Personally, I prefer to...

As for me, I am in high favor of the former (latter). Personally, I side with the former (latter) Frankly speaking/ To be frank/ To be honest/ Honestly speaking, it is the former (latter) that I approve of… adopt the former / latter approach

  1. There is no immediate solution to the problem of..., but... might be helpful/beneficial.

  2. No easy method can be at hand to solve the problem of ..., but the general awareness of the necessity/importance of... might be the first step on the right way.

  3. There is little doubt/denying that ...

  4. It is, therefore, obvious/evident that the task of...require immediate attention.

  5. In short, should learn to ; Only in this way can the most difficult problems be solved properly.
